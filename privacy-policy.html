Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Linger is a private app for sharing memories with people you trust. We only collect what we need to make the app work, your memories are only visible to users you specifically invite, and we do not sell your personal data or your content — ever.

This Privacy Policy for William Bauman ("we," "us," or "our") describes how and why we collect, store, use, and share your personal information when you use Linger, our mobile application (collectively, the "Services").

If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. If you have any questions, please contact us at lingersupport@gmail.com.

Summary of Key Points

What personal information do we process? We collect personal information you provide (such as your email, username, password, and the photos and videos you upload). We also collect limited technical data automatically.

Do we process sensitive personal information? No.

Do we collect information from third parties? No.

How do we process your information? To provide and improve the Services, communicate with you, ensure security, and comply with law.

With whom do we share personal information? Only with service providers that help us operate Linger, and only as described in this policy. We do not sell your data.

How do we keep your information safe? Through reasonable technical and organizational security measures, though no system is 100% secure.

What are your rights? Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to opt out of certain processing.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Disclose to Us

In Short: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us.

We collect the following personal information that you provide when you register for or use the Services:

  • Usernames
  • Passwords (stored in hashed form)
  • Email addresses
  • Display names
  • Profile images (if you choose to upload one)
  • Photos and videos you upload to memories
  • Memory titles, descriptions, and event dates you create
  • Support messages you send to us

Sensitive Information. We do not collect or process sensitive personal information.

User-Generated Content

When you upload photos or videos to a memory, this content is stored on our servers and shared only with users invited to that specific memory. Only users who have been invited to and accepted membership in a memory can view its photos and videos. You should only upload content you are comfortable sharing with the other members of that memory. You retain ownership of any content you upload — we do not claim rights to your photos or videos.

Application Data

If you use our mobile application, we may request access or permission to certain features from your mobile device, including:

  • Camera Access. To allow you to take photos and videos directly within Linger.
  • Photo Library Access. To allow you to upload existing photos and videos from your device to memories.
  • Push Notifications. To send you notifications about memory activity, friend requests, and invites. You can opt out in your device settings.

You may revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

Information Automatically Collected

In Short: Some information — such as your IP address and device characteristics — is collected automatically when you use our Services.

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Services, including:

  • Log and Usage Data. IP address, device type, operating system, app version, date and time of access, and actions taken within the app.
  • Device Information. Device identifiers, device model, and hardware settings — used for security, troubleshooting, and sending push notifications.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

In Short: We process your information to provide, improve, and administer the Services, to communicate with you, for security, and to comply with law.

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Services, including creating and managing your account, storing your memories, and displaying content to members of memories you belong to.
  • To authenticate users through email and password, verification codes, and session tokens.
  • To send transactional communications such as verification codes, password reset emails, and support responses.
  • To send push notifications about activity relevant to you (with your permission).
  • To respond to support requests and user inquiries.
  • To protect the Services against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • To identify usage trends so we can improve the Services.
  • To comply with legal obligations where required.
  • To protect the vital interests of users or others in emergency situations.

4. When and With Whom Do We Share Your Information?

In Short: We share information only in specific situations with specific third parties. We do not sell your data.

We may share your personal information in the following situations:

  • With Other Members of Your Memories. Photos, videos, memory details, and your display name are visible to other users you have invited to or who have been invited to a memory with you.
  • With Service Providers. We share information with vendors that help us operate the Services — including cloud hosting providers, email delivery providers, push notification services, and database providers. These providers are contractually required to protect your information.
  • For Legal Reasons. We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.
  • Business Transfers. If we are involved in a merger, sale, or acquisition, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change.
  • With Your Consent. We may share information with your explicit consent for other purposes.

5. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

In Short: We keep your information only as long as necessary to provide the Services or comply with legal obligations.

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal information from our active databases within a reasonable period, typically within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain information to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.

Photos and videos you upload to memories are retained as long as the memory exists. When a memory is deleted, its content is deleted from our active databases. Some residual data may remain in backup systems for a limited period before being fully removed.

6. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

In Short: We protect your information through reasonable technical and organizational security measures.

We implement appropriate security measures designed to protect your personal information, including:

  • Password hashing using industry-standard algorithms
  • Encrypted transmission of data (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access controls limiting who can view user data
  • Regular security reviews of our systems

However, no electronic transmission or storage system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee that unauthorized third parties will never defeat our security measures. You access the Services at your own risk.

Linger is intended for users 18 years of age or older, or the equivalent age of majority in your jurisdiction. By using the Services, you represent that you are at least 18, or that you are the parent or guardian of a minor who uses the Services with your consent.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. 9. Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Most browsers and some mobile operating systems include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") feature. Because no uniform technology standard for recognizing DNT signals has been finalized, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals. If a standard is adopted in the future, we will update this Policy accordingly.
